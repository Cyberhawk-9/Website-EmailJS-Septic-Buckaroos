## Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **Dimensions**: Minimum 1200px width for hero images, 800px width for content images
- **Quality**: High quality, professional appearance
- **Style**: Professional septic industry photography, clear and well-lit
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 500KB for content images, under 1MB for hero images)

## Notes

- All images should portray professional septic services
- Avoid stock photos that look too generic - industry-specific imagery preferred
- Photos showing actual septic work, equipment, and systems are ideal
- Maintain consistent quality and professional appearance across all images
